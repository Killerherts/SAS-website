About Us

Select All Services is a computer repair business that is here to solve all your computer needs. From data recovery to website design, Select All Services is here to help you.

History

Originally formed in Kansas City in 2013, Select All Services has been providing computer support in many forms for several years. Now located in Sacramento, California, Select All Services is here to serve you both in-person and remotely.

Nathan Hughes is the founder and owner of Select All Services. From a young age, he was always interested in technology and spent years understanding how computers work. Nathan has studied computer science at Kansas University, Kansas State, and Serria Community College with an emphasis on cybersecurity. He is always excited to discover new technologies and share his passion with his customers helping them feel more competent in their everyday technology needs.
